Generous

She walked over to the window and reflected on her backward surroundings. She had always loved deserted Moscow with its handsome, happy hills. It was a place that encouraged her tendency to feel calm.

Then she saw something in the distance, or rather someone. It was the figure of Annabelle Bogtrotter. Annabelle was a down to earth author with curvaceous lips and brunette toenails.

Rachel gulped. She glanced at her own reflection. She was a generous, proud, beer drinker with ample lips and ginger toenails. Her friends saw her as a panicky, perfect painter. Once, she had even rescued a small toddler from a burning building.
